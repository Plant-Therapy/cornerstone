Planting Kindness

At Plant Therapy, our goal is to have the greatest possible impact on the most people. Since the beginning, Plant Therapy owners and staff have placed donations, service work, and local support at the core of what we do and how we operate. Each year, we set aside a portion of the proceeds from each unit sold, to fund philanthropic local and national causes.
